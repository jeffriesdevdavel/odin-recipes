Scrambled Eggs

A classic, easy-to-make breakfast dish, scrambled eggs are fluffy, creamy, and can be customized with your favorite additions. Perfect for a quick and nutritious start to your day.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp milk or cream (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp butter or oil

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, milk (if using), salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Melt butter or heat oil in a non-stick skillet over medium-low heat.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the hot skillet.
  4. Let the eggs set for a few seconds, then gently push the cooked portions from the edges towards the center with a spatula.
  5. Continue cooking and folding until the eggs are mostly set but still moist.
  6. Remove from heat immediately and serve warm.
