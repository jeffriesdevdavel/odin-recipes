Peanut Butter Sandwich

A timeless and satisfying snack or light meal, the peanut butter sandwich is simple to prepare and loved by many. It's a quick source of protein and energy.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp jelly or jam (optional)

Instructions

  1. Take two slices of your favorite bread.
  2. Spread an even layer of peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  3. If using, spread jelly or jam on the other slice of bread.
  4. Place the two slices of bread together, peanut butter and jelly sides facing inwards.
  5. Cut in half if desired and enjoy immediately!
