Tierra de cacao

Tierra de cacao is a delicious chocolate mousse that is made with high-quality cocoa powder and other ingredients. It is a rich and creamy dessert that is perfect for chocolate lovers. The mousse is typically made by combining cocoa powder, sugar, eggs, and cream to create a smooth and velvety texture. It can be served chilled and is often garnished with whipped cream or chocolate shavings for an extra indulgent touch. Tierra de cacao is a popular dessert in many cultures and is enjoyed by people of all ages.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup heavy cream

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the cocoa powder and sugar until well combined.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs until they are light and fluffy.
  3. Gradually add the cocoa powder mixture to the eggs, beating until well combined.
  4. In another bowl, whip the heavy cream until it forms stiff peaks.
  5. Gently fold the whipped cream into the cocoa mixture until fully incorporated.
  6. Divide the mousse into serving dishes and refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.
  7. Garnish with whipped cream or chocolate shavings if desired, and enjoy!
